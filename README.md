Since snapchat is making memories a paid for storage I created this simple python tool to sort your memories! all you have to do is download your data from snapchat. This tool requires no login, it just sorts your memories into year and month folders.

Step 1 - Download your data from snapchat:
for more information read this article from snapchat: https://help.snapchat.com/hc/en-us/articles/7012305371156-How-do-I-download-my-data-from-Snapchat

1. Go to: https://accounts.snapchat.com/ and log into your account

2. click on "My Data"
<img width="326" height="460" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-11 171041" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8544d00c-ccd7-4b6b-93d4-5cb2707f4fb1" />

3. From this list, select the "Export your memories" switch and click on the button "Request Only Memories"
<img width="949" height="654" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-11 171429" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d7e4e158-2cd4-4a37-a845-596fb2764f6c" />

4. Make sure you select "All Time" from the date range window. Confirm your email and then click submit in the bottom right.
<img width="959" height="598" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-11 171641" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4fb1dc66-2764-44f0-a639-89c6993985a5" />

This process can take a couple of days for snapchat to send you your memories. It will arrive in an email. Next thing to do is unzip the folder named "memories" into the folder that this program is in and run the .exe.
